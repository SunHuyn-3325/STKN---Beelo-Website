Beelo Magic 3-in-1 Beeswax Pencils Jumbo Triangles 12pk

For confident creators ready to explore bigger ideas, Beelo Magic 3-in-1 Jumbo Triangles 12pk are the ultimate beeswax colouring pencils for kids. Made with a beeswax-based core and a clever triangular design, these longer non-toxic pencils offer more control, bolder colour, and three playful ways to create.

  • The purity of beeswax, now in a pencil: Beelo pencils are the first pencils made with a natural beeswax core. Made without petroleum-derived paraffin wax, they are non-toxic and safe for toddlers aged 1+.
  • Jumbo triangle shape supports grip development: Each pencil is 6 inches long and 0.24 inches in diameter in an ergonomic triangular shape. Helps to support pencil grip development in older toddlers and preschoolers.
  • Can be used in many fun ways: These pencils can be used with water for watercolour paint-like colours, or on paper, card, or even glass — giving kids lots of ways to play and create.
  • Easy colouring with minimal pressure: Many coloured pencils need hard pressure to show colour, which can be tough for little hands. Beelo' thick beeswax core makes colouring easy, with bright colours showing up easily.
  • Easy to clean: Simply wipes away from smooth surfaces with a damp cloth, for no mess creative play.
  • Give the gift of early creative play: Will make a thoughtful gift for your little artist, boys and girls alike. Whether as a stocking stuffer, birthday surprise, or everyday creative gift.
  • Safe for play & safe for planet: Made with FSC-certified wood and sustainable beeswax, Beelo Jumbo Triangle Pencils are a mindful choice for environmentally-conscious families.
  • Handcrafted by families, for families: Designed by parents who believe in safe, sustainable tools that unlock creativity at every stage.
